Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Exercise Science or related field

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's Degree in Exercise Science provides a strong foundation in anatomy, physiology, kinesiology, and exercise prescription. It prepares students for further education in physical therapy.

Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) Degree

Duration: 3 years

The Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree is the minimum requirement for entry-level practice as a physical therapist. It includes coursework in biomechanics, neuroscience, pharmacology, and clinical practice.

Clinical Residency (Optional)

Duration: 1-2 years

Some physical therapists choose to complete a clinical residency program to gain specialized training in areas such as orthopedics, neurology, or sports rehabilitation. Residencies provide additional hands-on experience and mentorship.
