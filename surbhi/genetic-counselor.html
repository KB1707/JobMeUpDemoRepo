Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Biology, Genetics, or a related field

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's Degree in a relevant field provides foundational knowledge in genetics, biology, and related sciences.

Master's Degree in Genetic Counseling

Duration: 2-3 years

A Master's Degree in Genetic Counseling provides specialized training in genetic counseling techniques, patient care, and ethical considerations.

Board Certification by the American Board of Genetic Counseling (ABGC)

Duration: Varies

Board certification demonstrates proficiency in genetic counseling and is often required for practice in clinical settings.
