Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Education (B.Ed.)

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's Degree in Education (B.Ed.) is the foundational degree for a teaching career. It provides comprehensive training in educational theory, teaching methodologies, and classroom management.

Master of Education (M.Ed.)

Duration: 1-2 years

A Master of Education (M.Ed.) is an advanced degree that allows teachers to specialize in areas such as curriculum development, educational leadership, or special education.

Teaching Certification

Duration: Varies by country

Obtaining a teaching certification typically involves completing a teacher preparation program and passing exams to demonstrate competency in teaching subject matter and pedagogy.
