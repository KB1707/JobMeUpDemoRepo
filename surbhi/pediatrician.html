Educational Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree in Pre-Medical Studies or Biology

    Duration: Typically 4 years

    A Bachelor's Degree in Pre-Medical Studies or Biology provides the foundational knowledge required for medical school.

  • Medical School Degree (MD)

    Duration: 4 years

    Medical school provides comprehensive training in medicine, including pediatric medicine.

  • Pediatric Residency Training

    Duration: 3 years

    After medical school, pediatricians undergo specialized residency training in pediatrics to gain hands-on experience in pediatric care.

  • Pediatric Board Certification (Optional)

    Duration: Varies

    Pediatricians may choose to become board-certified by passing examinations administered by the American Board of Pediatrics.

  • Continuing Medical Education

    Duration: Ongoing

    Pediatricians engage in continuing medical education to stay updated with the latest advancements in pediatric medicine.
