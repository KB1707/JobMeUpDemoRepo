Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree (Pre-Chiropractic Studies)

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's degree in pre-chiropractic studies provides a foundation in biology, chemistry, physics, and other sciences necessary for chiropractic education.

Doctor of Chiropractic (D.C.) Degree

Duration: 4 years

A Doctor of Chiropractic (D.C.) degree program focuses on chiropractic principles, spinal manipulation, diagnosis, and patient care through hands-on clinical experience.

Licensure and Certification

Duration: Varies by jurisdiction

Chiropractors must obtain a state license to practice, which typically requires passing national and state board exams. Optional certifications are available for specialized areas of practice.
