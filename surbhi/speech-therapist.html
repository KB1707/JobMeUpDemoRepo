Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Communication Sciences and Disorders

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's Degree in Communication Sciences and Disorders provides foundational knowledge in speech, language, and hearing sciences, preparing students for graduate studies in speech therapy or related fields.

Master's Degree in Speech-Language Pathology

Duration: 2-3 years

A Master's Degree in Speech-Language Pathology is the minimum requirement for licensure and certification as a speech therapist. It includes coursework in speech disorders, language development, and clinical practice.

Clinical Fellowship (CFY) and State Licensure

Duration: Varies

After completing a master's program, speech therapists must complete a clinical fellowship under the supervision of a licensed speech-language pathologist. They must also obtain state licensure to practice independently.
