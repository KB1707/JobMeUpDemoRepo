Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree (Pre-Medical Studies)

Duration: Typically 4 years

Completion of a Bachelor's degree with a focus on pre-medical studies, including courses in biology, chemistry, physics, and mathematics.

Medical Degree (MD or DO)

Duration: 4 years

Graduation from an accredited medical school program, which includes classroom study and clinical rotations in various medical specialties.

Residency Training in Chosen Specialty

Duration: 3-7 years (depending on specialty)

Completion of a residency program in a chosen medical specialty, providing hands-on training and supervised clinical experience.
