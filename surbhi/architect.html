Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Architecture (B.Arch)

Duration: Typically 5 years

A Bachelor's degree in Architecture provides comprehensive training in architectural design, theory, history, and technical skills.

Master of Architecture (M.Arch)

Duration: 1-3 years (depending on previous education)

The Master of Architecture degree offers advanced study in architectural design, research, and specialization in specific areas of interest.

Architectural License

Duration: Varies by jurisdiction

Architects must obtain a license to practice, which typically involves completing a period of practical experience and passing the Architect Registration Examination.
