Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Rehabilitation Counseling

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's Degree in Rehabilitation Counseling provides foundational knowledge in counseling theory, human development, and disability studies. Students also gain practical skills through internships and practicum experiences.

Master's Degree in Rehabilitation Counseling

Duration: 2-3 years

A Master's Degree in Rehabilitation Counseling is the minimum requirement for entry-level practice. It includes coursework in counseling techniques, assessment, case management, and vocational rehabilitation.

Professional Certification and Licensure

Duration: Varies

After completing a master's program, rehabilitation counselors may pursue professional certification, such as the Certified Rehabilitation Counselor (CRC) credential. Some states also require licensure for practice.
