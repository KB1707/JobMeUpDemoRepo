Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Law or Pre-law Studies

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's degree in law or pre-law studies provides a foundational understanding of legal principles, history, and processes.

Law School (Juris Doctor - J.D.)

Duration: 3 years

Law school educates students in critical thinking, legal analysis, and advocacy skills necessary for practicing law. Graduates earn a Juris Doctor (J.D.) degree.

Bar Examination

Duration: Varies by jurisdiction

After completing law school, aspiring lawyers must pass the bar examination in the jurisdiction where they intend to practice to obtain a license to practice law.
