Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Nursing (BSN)

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's Degree in Nursing (BSN) is the foundational degree for a nursing career. It provides comprehensive training in nursing theory, practice, and clinical skills.

Master of Science in Nursing (MSN)

Duration: 2-3 years

A Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) is an advanced degree that allows nurses to specialize in areas such as nurse practitioner, nurse educator, or nurse administrator.

Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP)

Duration: 3-4 years (after MSN)

The Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) is the highest level of nursing education, focusing on leadership, advanced clinical practice, and evidence-based practice.
